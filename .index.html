About Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna is one of the most loved deities in Hinduism. He is known for his wisdom, kindness, flute music, and teachings from the Bhagavad Gita.

Krishna teaches us the path of devotion, truth, and righteousness.

Teachings of Krishna

Truth

Always follow truth and righteousness in life.

Devotion

Pure devotion leads the soul towards peace.

Wisdom

Knowledge removes fear and confusion.
